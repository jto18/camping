THE CAMPING GUIDE

I had never been camping before. The first time it was a mess. It was very cold and snowing in spring. I didn't have all the right camping stuff that is why it is important for you to read this tips so you can enjoy, relax and have more time for adventures.
I have no RV recommendations for now, as a tent camper, you have likely developed a certain image of camping. Sleeping on the ground, waking up to the sounds of nature, cooking over an open fire, and relaxing in the woods all come to mind.
Why I go camping if there is a lot of bugs and wild animals? The benefits are more important and lucky for me I didn't have an encounter with wild animals. The reasons are:

  1. To spend time with friends and family.
  2. Find silence and solitude.
  3. Get away from a screen.
  4. Enjoy quality time with kids and pets, mental and physical health,
    quality time for relationships and the feeling of accomplishment.
